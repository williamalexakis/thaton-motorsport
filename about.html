About

We are Θaton Motorsport, a team of six students aged 15 – 17 from St. Catherines British school competing in STEM Racing – the world's leading STEM competition (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics).

We design, test, and race high-performance miniature F1-style cars using industry-standard engineering tools and creative design processes.

Guided by our core values of Teamwork, Dedication, Innovation, and Performance, we aim to advance through the competition and earn our place at the STEM Racing World Finals, proudly representing our School and Greece on the global STEM stage.
