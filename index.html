WHO ARE WE?

We are Θaton Motorsport, a team of six students aged 15 – 17 from St. Catherines British school in Athens, Greece, competing in the globally-acclaimed STEM Racing competition.

WHAT IS STEM RACING?

STEM Racing (formerly F1 in Schools) is a global competition in which teams of students design, test, and race miniature F1-style cars powered by CO2 canisters.

Teams also build their own unique brand and promote it through websites, social media, and advertisements, in order to attain essential sponsorships that fund the project.

The aim of STEM Racing is to allow students to develop skillsets that extend beyond classrooms, by utilizing real-world tools and techniques to advance their careers in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics).
